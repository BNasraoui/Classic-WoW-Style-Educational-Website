Paladin

Description

Much like the priest, the paladin worships the light, defending the world from the many enemies of the light. This holy crusader uses a mixture of holy spells and melee weapons to protect and aid allies, as well as defend against malevolent attackers. If you find yourself an enemy of the light, be prepared. The Paladin is a strong combatant and a dangerous enemy to have.[1]
