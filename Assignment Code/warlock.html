Warlock

Description

Traveller, we start our journey today in the depths of the inferno, a place home to people who have turned from the light and embraced the darkness. Our first class today, the Warlock, has not just accepted this darkness but harnessed it and crafted it to do their bidding. The Warlock summons demonic minions like imps, voidwalkers, succubi and the fel hunters to help them gain the upper hand in fights. While they don’t directly serve any evil, they embrace their magic to further their own motives.[1]
