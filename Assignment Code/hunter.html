Hunter

Description

The final class in the realm of purgatory is the Hunter. The Hunter a survivalist and woodsman that tames the beasts of the wild to fight alongside them. The Hunter places traps on the battlefield to ensnare their enemies and keep them at range or crowd control them to keep them from their allies. Hunters are expert duellists so be careful on your journey not to make an enemy of one.[1]
