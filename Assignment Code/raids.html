Raids

What is a Raid?

A raid is a 40-man instance consisting of multiple bosses that progressively increase in difficulty. 40 people group together to defeat some of the most difficult content in the game for which they are awarded some of the most powerful loot in the game. Raids initially can take groups 2-3 4 hour days of progression in classic raids, but become progressively easier to complete as people get more gear and in turn become stronger. To start raiding you have to be level 60 and usually groups prefer people to have gotten some armour and weapons from dungeons so it’s recommended to do some dungeons first before looking to join in on your friend’s raids.[1]
