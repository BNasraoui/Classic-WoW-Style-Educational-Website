Priest

Description

We have finally made it to Paradise, traveller. We’re through the worst of it and we can at least for a moment relax. The first class of Paradise is the Priest. Priests are worshippers of the light and specialise healing their allies, but with light comes shadow and some priests choose to specialise in the shadow becoming powerful self-reliant damage dealers. Priests intentions within the world is the proliferation and worship of the light and will aid and protect their allies whenever they can.[1]
