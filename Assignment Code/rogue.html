Rogue

Description

Our Second Class of the Inferno is a dark and mischievous one. The Rogue hides in plain sight using their power of invisibility to blend into the surroundings. The Rogue being a master poisoner, coats their weapons with deadly poisons to apply ticking damage to their enemies to gain the upper hand in combat. The Rogue is an opportunist and will always strake from the shadows. Be aware traveller, their could be enemy Rogues hiding anywhere throughout your journey so always be ready for a fight and to know when to retreat when facing certain death.[1]
