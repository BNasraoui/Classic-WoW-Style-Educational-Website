Druid

Description

The final class of paradise and the end of our journey, traveller. After this you will be ready to venture out into Azeroth and begin your journey. There are no right or wrong answers and everyone’s journey is their own, but still choose wisely, traveller. The Druid is a shape shifter, one with nature they are able to shift forms to perform all sorts of roles to suit their needs. They can transform into bears and defend their allies, cats and stalk their prey from the shadows, Great owl-like creatures called moonkins to perform ranged attacks, and trees of life to heal their allies.[1]
