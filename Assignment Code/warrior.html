Warrior

Description

The second class of the realm of purgatory is the Warrior, a master of weapons capable of wielding any weapon in the world but specialising in swords and axes. The warrior is capable of dealing great damage as well as withstanding it which makes them such a worthwhile ally and a dangerous foe.[1]
