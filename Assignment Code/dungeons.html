Dungeons

What is a Dungeon?

A Dungeon is a 5 man instance where players are tasked to defeat a series of bosses and are rewarded armour, weapon, and trinkets from successful boss kills. Dungeons are scattered all around Azeroth and more become available the higher your level. 5 man dungeon groups usually have 1 Tank to hold threat on enemies and tank their damage, 1 Healer to heal the group, and 3 Damage characters. Dungeons in Classic WoW are mostly done while levelling or to gear up before raids.[1]
