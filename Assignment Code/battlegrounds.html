Battlegrounds

What Are Battlegrounds?

Battle grounds are player versus player instances where you fight with your faction against the enemy faction to achieve some objective. The 3 Battlegrounds available in Classic Wow are Arathi Basin, Alterac Valley, and Warsong Gulch.[1]

Arathi Basin
Arathi Basin is a 15 man a side instance where players compete against the enemy faction for resources players collect by tapping nodes. The first faction to successfully capture 2000 resources wins.[1]

Alterac Valley
Alterac Valley is a 40 man a side instance where you fight against the enemy faction over map control in the Alterac Mountains. Players push up from their home base destroying enemy faction tower and controlling their grave sites until they are able to take the enemy faction’s base. The battleground is won when one faction’s general has been killed.[1]

Warsong Gulch
Warsong Gulch is a 10 man a side instance where players attempt to capture the enemy team’s flag. The first to successfully capture the enemy team’s flag 3 times wins.[1]
