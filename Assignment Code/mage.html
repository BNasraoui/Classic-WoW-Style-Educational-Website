Mage

Description

Well done, traveller. We’ve made it through the Inferno and reached Purgatory. Purgatory exists as a plane in between paradise and the inferno to hold those who are not necessarily evil but don’t have the noblest of intentions either. The first class of Purgatory is the Mage. The Mage is a student of all things magic and can specialise in either frost, arcane, or fire magic. Mages intentions are not necessarily good or evil, they value knowledge above all else and will stop at nothing to expand their understanding of all things magical in nature.[1]
