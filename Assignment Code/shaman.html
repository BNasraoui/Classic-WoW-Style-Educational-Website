Shaman

Description

The Third class as we prepare to leave l'inferno is the Shaman. The Shaman, a primitive spiritualist, bends the elements to their will heal their allies and harm their foes. The Shaman is a hybrid class capable of helping and harming people with their many totems they place around the battlefield as well as the many spells in their arsenal.[1]
